# GlacierNET-KZ Stakeholder Outreach Pack

Цель: быстро получить customer discovery, пилотный интерес и LOI для Astana Hub / AI'preneurs / climate grants.

## One-line pitch

GlacierNET-KZ is an AI climate-risk intelligence platform that turns Sentinel-2/Landsat satellite imagery into annual glacier maps, water-risk indicators, forecasts, and decision reports for Kazakhstan.

## 8-week pilot proposal

**Pilot area:** Ili Alatau, Tuyuksu and Bogdanovich glacier zone  
**Duration:** 8 weeks  
**Deliverables:**

- dashboard with 2000-2024 glacier history;
- annual glacier area report with uncertainty/confidence flags;
- PDF decision report for non-technical stakeholders;
- API/MCP access for analysts;
- final presentation with recommendations.

**Success metrics:**

- stakeholder can identify high-risk years and caveats in under 3 minutes;
- report generation time reduced from manual GIS work to automated dashboard export;
- data quality and uncertainty are visible for every year;
- at least one glacier/watershed use case is validated by domain expert.
